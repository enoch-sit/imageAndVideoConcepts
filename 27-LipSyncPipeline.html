Real-time feasibility verdict
Total offline (non-streaming) pipeline: ~8–15 s for a 10 s clip (0.8–1.5× real-time). The bottleneck is the diffusion renderer (EchoMimic/SadTalker/DAWN): ~5–10 s/step × 20 DDPM steps. For real-time usage, reduce DDPM steps to 6–10 and use a lightweight renderer with direct blendshape-to-FLAME mapping (bypassing diffusion). Whisper + MFA + Phoneme→Viseme→Blendshape + One-Euro combined takes <300 ms, well within real-time budget.
