SadTalker — Audio-Driven
3D Talking Head

SadTalker generates realistic talking-head animations from a single portrait photo and an audio clip. It regresses 3D Morphable Model (3DMM) coefficients from audio, then maps them to head-pose and expression motion fields for image animation.

01

3D Morphable Model Coefficients

SadTalker represents a face state as a 257-dim 3DMM coefficient vector based on the BFM (Basel Face Model). This separates identity, expression, texture, lighting, and pose into disentangled subspaces that can be predicted from audio independently.

BFM Coefficient Breakdown

3DMM decomposition (BFM) S = S̄ + A_id·α + A_exp·β_exp
S̄ ∈ ℝ^(N×3) — mean face shape
α ∈ ℝ⁸⁰ — identity shape PCA (80 dims)
β_exp ∈ ℝ⁶⁴ — expression coefficients (64 dims)

Full coeff vector c = [α(80), β_exp(64),
β_tex(80), γ(27), p(6)]
Total = 257 dimensions
02

Audio → 3DMM Motion

SadTalker uses two separate networks to go from audio to motion: PoseVAE regresses head pose variations (yaw/pitch/roll + translation, 6D motion) and ExpNet regresses expression coefficients. Audio features are Mel spectrograms processed by a 1D-CNN encoder.

PoseVAE — Head Motion Prediction

SadTalker uses a variational autoencoder conditioned on audio to generate head pose sequences. The pose is represented as 6D: yaw, pitch, roll (head rotation) + tx, ty, tz (3D translation of the head in the 3DMM camera space). The VAE sampling adds natural variation — preventing all heads from moving identically to the same audio.

PoseVAE sampling z ~ q(z|c_id) — sample from identity-conditioned prior
p_t = PoseVAE_decoder(z, audio_feat_t)
p_t = [yaw, pitch, roll, tx, ty, tz]_t ∈ ℝ⁶

ExpNet — Expression Coefficient Prediction

ExpNet regresses the 64-dim expression coefficients β_exp from the audio feature sequence using a transformer encoder. Only expression-related 3DMM coefficients are predicted (not identity or texture); the identity coefficients are frozen from the input portrait image.

ExpNet β_exp_t = ExpNet(DeepSpeech2_feat_t, c_id) ∈ ℝ⁶⁴
audio_feat = DeepSpeech2(mel_t:t+W) ∈ ℝ²⁹
Window W = 16 audio frames = ~200 ms

Full Pipeline Overview

SadTalker's full pipeline: (1) extract identity coefficients from the portrait image, (2) audio→motion (PoseVAE + ExpNet), (3) render expression-motion fields, (4) image animation via face-vid2vid (FaceVid2Vid).

Course usage context — comparison with DAWN and EchoMimic
SadTalker (App 19), DAWN (App 20) and EchoMimic (App 21) all take audio + portrait → video but differ architecturally. SadTalker uses an explicit 3DMM intermediate; DAWN uses single-image diffusion with noise-injected temporal motion; EchoMimic conditions a video diffusion model on audio embeddings and reference face frames directly, without a 3DMM stage.
