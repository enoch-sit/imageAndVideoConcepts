Design Philosophy
Each app is self-contained but intellectually dependent on earlier concepts. A student who understands Apps 01-05 (foundations + diffusion mathematics) will grasp Apps 15-18 (video diffusion) much more readily. The dependency graph makes these pre-requisite chains explicit, allowing students to identify gaps in their understanding before attempting later apps.
